OVERVIEW

We're not in the wing business. We're in the flavor business.
It's been our mission to serve the world flavor since we first opened shop in '94, and we're just getting started. 1997 saw the opening of our first franchised Willy's Wings location, and by 2002 we had served the world one billion wings. It's flavor that defines us and has made Wingstop one of the fastest growing brands in the restaurant industry. Wingstop is the destination for made-to-crave wings and flavorful chicken sandwich, hand-cut seasoned fries and any of our famous sides.
For people who demand flavor in everything they do, there's only Willy's Wings- because it's more than a meal, it's a flavor experience.
