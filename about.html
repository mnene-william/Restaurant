Our History


Wingstop was founded in 1994 in Garland, Texas, by Antonio Swad and his partner, who wanted to create a restaurant that specialized in chicken wings. The first location was a small takeout joint that quickly gained popularity for its flavorful wings and unique sauces. In 1997, the first franchised location opened in California, marking the beginning of Wingstop's expansion beyond Texas. By 2002, the brand had served its one billionth wing, solidifying its status as a leader in the chicken wing industry. Today, Wingstop has over 1,500 locations worldwide and continues to innovate with new flavors and menu items.
Wingstop's success can be attributed to its commitment to quality ingredients, made-to-order wings, and a focus on customer experience. The brand has also embraced technology, offering online ordering and delivery options to meet the demands of modern consumers. With a strong presence in both the United States and international markets, Wingstop remains dedicated to its mission of serving the world flavor, one wing at a time.

Our Mission


At Wingstop, our mission is to serve the world flavor. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality food, made-to-order and tossed in a variety of bold and unique flavors. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our food; we strive to create a welcoming and enjoyable dining experience for every guest. We believe in using only the freshest ingredients and cooking our wings to perfection, ensuring that each bite is packed with flavor. As we continue to grow and expand, we remain focused on our core values of quality, service, and community involvement. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or ordering online, our mission is to deliver an unforgettable flavor experience that keeps you coming back for more.
