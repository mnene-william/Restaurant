Our History & Mission

Willy's Wings was founded in 1994 by a group of friends who shared a passion for great food and good times. The first restaurant opened in Dallas, Texas, and quickly became a local favorite. The founders wanted to create a place where people could come together to enjoy delicious wings, flavorful sauces, and a fun atmosphere. Over the years, Willy's Wings has expanded to locations across the country, but the original spirit of the brand remains the same: to serve up mouthwatering wings and create unforgettable memories.



Willy's Wings is not just about the food; it's about the experience. The founders believed that great food should be enjoyed in a fun and lively environment. That's why each Willy's Wings location is designed to be a place where friends and family can gather, relax, and enjoy each other's company. From the vibrant decor to the friendly staff, every aspect of Willy's Wings is designed to create a welcoming and enjoyable dining experience.
